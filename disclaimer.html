Tax Disclaimer

Last updated: June 11, 2026

No Tax, Legal, or Accounting Advice

STR Tracker (the "App") is a record-keeping tool only. Nothing in the App — including hour thresholds, progress indicators, labels such as "Met" or "Not yet," reports, or any other content — constitutes tax, legal, or accounting advice, and nothing in the App creates an accountant-client or attorney-client relationship.

Thresholds Are Simplified Summaries

References to figures such as "750 hours" (real estate professional status) or "100 hours" (a material participation test) are simplified summaries of complex provisions of the U.S. Internal Revenue Code and Treasury Regulations (including IRC §469 and related regulations). Whether you qualify for any tax status or deduction depends on many factors the App does not and cannot evaluate, including the nature of your activities, participation of other persons, average rental periods, and your overall tax situation. Tax law and its interpretation change frequently.

Your Records, Your Responsibility

You are solely responsible for the accuracy, completeness, and truthfulness of every entry you record. The App displays and formats the data you provide; it does not verify it. The IRS and courts decide what documentation is sufficient — generating a report with this App does not guarantee that your records will be accepted in an audit or any other proceeding.

Consult a Professional

Before taking any tax position, including positions related to short-term rental activities, material participation, or real estate professional status, consult a qualified tax professional (CPA, enrolled agent, or tax attorney) who can evaluate your individual circumstances.
