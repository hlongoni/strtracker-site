STR Tracker

STR Tracker is an iPhone app that helps short-term rental owners keep contemporaneous records of the hours they spend on rental activities, attach supporting evidence, and export organized reports for their tax professional.

STR Tracker is a record-keeping tool only — it does not provide tax, legal, or accounting advice.
